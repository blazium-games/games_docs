# Graphical Assets Guidelines

When listing your product to blazium.games, you will be required to provide various graphical assets.
These are displayed in different places on blazium.games.

:::info[File Formats]

All files must formatted as PNG or JPG.

:::

## Thumbnail Image
- **Size:** 1280x720.
- **Usage:** Used when listing the product in the home page, browse page or similar pages.
- **Design:** For best results use the product's key art and logo.

![image](/img/graphical_assets_examples/thumbnail.png)

## Cover Image
- **Size:** 640x360.
- **Usage:** Used in the product page side bar.
- **Design:** The product's logo should be easily legible **even at smaller sizes**.

![image](/img/graphical_assets_examples/cover.png)

## Screenshots Images
- **Size:** 1920x1080 minimum, 16:9 aspect ratio.
- **Usage:** Screenshots are displayed on your store page. You must provide at least 4 screenshots of your product.
- **Design:** Screenshots should exclusively show the usage of the product (gameplay, tool or asset being used).

![image](/img/graphical_assets_examples/screenshot.png)
